Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Yakisoba noodles
  • 3 Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2-3 Tbsp Sesame oil
  • Tofu
  • Half a Garlic
  • Half an Onion
  • 1 Tbsp Chili oil
  • 1-2 Tbsp Teriyaki sauce

Step 1

Heat up a frying pan with some Sesame oil, once hot enough, start cooking onion and garlic until they start to brown.

Step 2

Start fyring tofu and add soy sauce and chili oil, fry until tofu is cooked to liking.

Step 3

Add noodles and teriyaki sauce and cover pan to steam noodles, cook until they are soft.

Step 4

Consume yummy noodles :)
